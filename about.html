About Us

Dekyil Guest House is a family owned and run hotel located in the heart of Bumthang and features a scenic view of the Bumthang valley and the Jakar Dzong. It is ideally located within walking distance of Chamkhar downtown area to indulge in souvenir shopping or a stroll around town.
The rooms are equipped with modern amenities including under-floor heating systems for the bathrooms, combined with an exceptional experience in Bhutanese hospitality.
